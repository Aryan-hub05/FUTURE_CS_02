## Objective
To identify phishing emails, analyze common phishing indicators, and provide awareness and prevention guidelines for users.

---

## Sample Phishing Email

From: support@paytm-secure-login.com  
Subject: Urgent! Verify Your Account

Dear User,  
Your Paytm account will be suspended within 24 hours.  
Please verify your account immediately using the link below:

http://paytm-login-alert.xyz

---

## Phishing Indicators Identified

1. Fake sender email domain  
2. Urgent and threatening language  
3. Suspicious external link  
4. Generic greeting  

---

## Email Classification

Status: Phishing  
Risk Level: High  

---

## Risk Analysis Table

| Indicator | Risk |
|---------|------|
| Fake Sender | High |
| Malicious Link | High |
| Urgency | Medium |
| Generic Greeting | Medium |

---

## User Awareness & Prevention Tips

- Check sender email addresses carefully
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Visit official websites directly
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Report phishing emails

---

## Conclusion
Phishing emails trick users using fear and fake identities. Awareness is the best defense.
